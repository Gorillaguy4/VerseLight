Fun Fact!

The shortest verse in the Bible is "Jesus wept." (John 11:35).

Did You Know?

The Bible was written over approximately 1,500 years by more than 40 different authors.

The original Bible manuscripts were written in Hebrew, Aramaic, and Greek.

Popular Bible Themes

Faith: Trusting God even when we can't see the whole picture.

Grace: God's unearned love and mercy toward us.

Salvation: The gift of eternal life through Jesus Christ.

Forgiveness: Letting go of anger and resentment, as God forgives us.

Top Bible Stories for Kids

Noah's Ark: God saves Noah and the animals from the flood.

David and Goliath: A young shepherd boy defeats a giant with faith and a sling.

Jesus Feeds 5000: Jesus performs a miracle by feeding thousands with five loaves and two fish.

The Good Samaritan: A story teaching love and kindness to others, even strangers.

"For God so loved the world, that He gave His only Son." — John 3:16

How to Study the Bible

Pray first: Ask God for understanding and wisdom.

Read regularly: Set aside daily time to read and reflect.

Use study tools: Commentaries, dictionaries, and concordances can help.

Join a group: Discussing with others deepens your understanding.

Bible Trivia

Q: Who was the oldest man mentioned in the Bible?
A: Methuselah, who lived 969 years.

Q: What was the first miracle Jesus performed?
A: Turning water into wine at a wedding in Cana.

Q: How many books are in the Bible?
A: 66 books (39 Old Testament, 27 New Testament).
